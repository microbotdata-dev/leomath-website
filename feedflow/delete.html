Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 2026

Important: Feedflow stores all data locally on your device only. There is no data on our servers to delete.

How to delete your data

Option 1 — Uninstall the app

  1. Open your phone Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Feedflow
  3. Tap Uninstall

This removes all locally stored photos, captions, schedules, and settings permanently.

Option 2 — Clear app data

  1. Open your phone Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Feedflow
  3. Tap Storage → Clear Data

Option 3 — Revoke Instagram access

  1. Open Instagram
  2. Go to Settings → Security → Apps and Websites
  3. Find Feedflow → tap Remove

This revokes the Instagram token stored on your device.
