Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary: Feedflow stores all data locally on your device only. We do not send data to external servers. Your Instagram token never leaves your phone.

1. Data We Collect

Feedflow stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Photos you add to the grid
  • Instagram access token (encrypted via flutter_secure_storage)
  • Post captions and hashtags
  • Scheduled post settings
  • App preferences

We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

2. Instagram / Facebook Login

Feedflow uses Facebook Login to authenticate with Instagram. Your access token is stored encrypted on your device only and is used solely to interact with Instagram's API on your behalf (reading your grid, publishing posts).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Meta / Instagram Graph API — for publishing posts and downloading your grid. Subject to Meta's Data Policy.
  • Google AdMob — for displaying ads in the free version. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play In-App Purchases — for Pro subscription.

4. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

5. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the Data Deletion page.

6. Children's Privacy

Feedflow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
